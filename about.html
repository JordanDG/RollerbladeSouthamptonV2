About Us:

We're 3 friends who've been roller-blading for 30 years together, we'd like to promote roller-blades in Southampton and surrounding areas. We'd like to offer training & coaching on rollerblades. All ages and skill levels welcome, With the first thirty minutes free of charge! Afterwards, rates are as follows:


  • Children (up to 16 years old): £10 / 45mins

  • Adults: £15 / 45mins


We're offering training in all types of roller-blades, including aggressive & inline. We'll soon be opening our YouTube channel, featuring tutorials & guides about roller-blading and maintaining roller-blades. Any questions? Feel free to contact us below!
